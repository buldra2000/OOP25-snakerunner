# OOP25-SnakeRunner

This game is an imitation of the game 'Snake Runner'. When the application is started, the menu will be show. You can choose:

- **Play:** Starts the game.
- **Option:** Shows checkbox for audio on/off.
- **Exit:** Closes the game.

The game consists of 4 levels:

- **Level 1** The player’s goal is to collect all the apples in order to access the next level.
- **Level 2** Walls are introduced as obstacles. The snake must move through them while avoiding collisions, or it will lose a life; the objective is to reach the flag to access the next level. Another object is the “clock,” which slows down time and allows the player to control the snake more precisely. After a few seconds, the effect wears off. In this level eating the apples gives only points.
- **Level 3** Apples still give points. The objective is to collect the key to open the door and pass through it to reach the flag. The level becomes more challenging, so in addition to the previous power-ups, there are green mushrooms that grant the player an extra life.
- **Level 4** The final level is the most difficult. Bombs are introduced, and colliding with them results in an instant game over, regardless of how many lives you have. The objective remains the same: collect keys to open doors and reach the flag.
 
**Enjoy SnakeRunner!**
